Crizzzz

Crizzzz is a self taught intermediate 3D artist and amateur 3D animator. Crizzzz has worked with XelionixGP and has made models for the game Harsh Habitats Harsh Habitats is an open world multiplayer survival game where you gather resources, build your village, and protect your village from offen raids by enemy villages. You face challenges based on the biome you settle in. The game offers weapons that were used by many different tribes. Crizzzz has also worked on Fred Opens A Refrigerator, an animated series on YouTube. called, Where the antagonist Fred (the main character of Harsh Habitats) Opens A refrigerator and would find something random and the episode would be based off of that item he found.
